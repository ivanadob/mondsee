Quattuor Evangelia (Ingolstadt Gospel Book)

Mondsee — s. VIII2

The original manuscript contained the four gospels preceded by Eusebian canon tables with sumptuous decoration. The now surviving leaves were used as binding waste for account books in the 17th and 19th centuries. A rough estimation of the layout and the amount of text fitting one page suggests the original manuscript had about 260-280 fols.

Selected literature: Bischoff 1980, pp. 18–19. — CLA IX, 1325
