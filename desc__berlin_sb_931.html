Berlin, Staatsbibliothek, Lat. qu. 931 (currently in Krakow, Biblioteka Jagiellońska)
Show TEI-XML
Ansegisus: Collectio capitularium

Parchment — 89 fols. — 275 × 195 mm — Alamannia (Mondsee?) — s. IX2 (around 900)

The manuscript was once held at the Staatsbibliothek in Berlin, from where it disappeared for years (probably after WWII). At the time Bischoff wrote the Schreibschule (1980) he had access only to photos of two leaves and the description made by Christ. The content of the MS is listed in Capitularia database

Selected literature: MS description in Capitularia - Edition der fränkischen Herrschererlasse
