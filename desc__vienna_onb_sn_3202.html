Lectionarium

Parchment — 13 fols. — — s. IX1

From the original manuscript survived 14 strips, which were used as binding waste in an early print belonging to the Mondsee library. The fragments were detached 1960 and mounted on goldbeater's skin to form 13 partial leaves.
